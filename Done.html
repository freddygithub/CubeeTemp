You're All Done!


If everything looks good, you are ready to print. Follow these tips:

  • Place the Special Cubee Backlit Film into your inkjet printer.
  • Make sure to print on the GLOSSY side of the film.
  • Once printing is complete, let stand for 1-2 minutes before cutting.
  • Slide photo into your Cubee and enjoy!
